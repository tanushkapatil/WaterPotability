About Water Potability

Water potability refers to whether water is safe for human consumption. This system uses machine learning to predict potability based on standard water quality parameters. For official water quality assessment, please consult certified laboratories.

Tip: Typical drinking water has pH between 6.5 and 8.5, hardness less than 120 mg/L, and TDS less than 500 ppm.
